About us.

HI!

We are Josie and Hollie and thanks for visiting Plenty Full ! Our company started out as
a small hobby between the two of us in our uni flat. We started posting pictures of our food
we were creating onto instagram. Slowly we started to grow a small following and by
the time we were graduating we realised we could turn our small hobby into a full time job
if we tried our hardest. We started small, creating a free to download e-book slowly
building up to where we are now.

The purpose of the workshops are to show people just how much variety is
out there when it comes to vegetarian food and we wanted the opportunity
to share that with everyone.

So wether you are vegetarian and hunting for recipes, wanting to learn how to
cooker you are just trying to find a sweet snack, we just want
to say thanks for coming to our small site and we hope you enjoy.

Joise & Hollie
